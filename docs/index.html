// About

Kevin is a product manager with an unconventional path built on responding to emergencies, caring for patients, and now building software that makes a difference in healthcare.

With a career that started in public safety, spanning 911 operator and firefighter/EMT—that foundation gave him a front-row seat to high-stakes decision-making and the gaps that exist when systems don't talk to each other.

Transitioning into nursing as a PICU and ER nurse, then hitting the road as a travel nurse during the COVID-19 pandemic, moving between hospital systems across the country and seeing firsthand where clinical workflows break down under pressure.

Now all of that is channeled into product, building AI-powered tools that solve real problems for the clinicians and patients he used to work alongside. Kevin believes AI, applied thoughtfully to healthcare, can close the gaps he's witnessed firsthand.
